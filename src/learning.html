Learn about Phishing Attacks

Learning about Phishing attacks is pretty essential because it is one of the most common and effective cyberattacks. They exploit human trust to steal sensitive information like passwords, financial details and personal data.

Understanding phishing empowers individuals to recognize deceptive tactics such as fake emails, malicious links, or fraudulent websites.
